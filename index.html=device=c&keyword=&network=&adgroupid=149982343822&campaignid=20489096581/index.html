How to Activate Amazon MyTV.

There are basically 3 main step processes to activating Amazon MyTV. These steps will depend on two key things, which are: If the application is installed and whether you have an Amazon account. And so, the three main step processes of activation include:

  • Registering an Amazon Account
  • Installing the Amazon Prime Video app
  • Activating Amazon MyTV
Note: all these steps are applicable if you don't have an Amazon account and if your TV doesn't have the App installed (In most devices, the App comes pre-installed). But if you have an account and App installed, you can simply skip to the activation part. With that said,

let's now discuss each step, starting with registering the account.

Creating an Amazon Account

  • 1. Start by heading to Amazon.com/MyTV: Open a web browser and click on the provided link, which will automatically take you to the Amazon.com/MyTV page.
  • 2. Click the 'Create Your Amazon Account' button on the sign-in page.
  • 3. On the registration page, enter your details: Name, Email or Mobile Number, and Password
  • 4. Click the 'Verify your mobile number' or 'Verify your email' option to proceed with verification, depending on the method you choose to use.
  • 5. To proceed, solve the puzzle by clicking the 'Solve the Puzzle' button and correctly selecting the answer.
  • 6. Retrieve the verification code sent to your mobile number or email, enter it into the provided field, and click 'Create Your Amazon Account' to finalize the registration process.

Installing the Amazon Prime Video app

Here are the installation steps:

  • 1. Firstly, connect your Smart TV to the Internet. This step is essential to ensure that your Smart TV can connect to the Internet, as the Amazon MyTV activation process requires an Internet connection. Therefore, ensure you have stable internet connectivity.
  • 2. Open the Home Menu on your Smart TV screen. Locate the Home Menu button or icon on your Smart TV's remote control or interface and press it to access the main menu.
  • 3. Go to App Store on your Smart TV Go to the App Store on your Smart TV and search for the "Amazon Prime Video" application. If the App is not pre-installed, download and install it from the store.

Activating Amazon MyTV

Now, the steps below will help you complete Amazon MyTV Activate:

  • 1. Locate and open the Amazon Prime Video app on your Smart TV. Go to Home Menu or use the dedicated app button on your remote control to find and open the Amazon Prime Video app.
  • 2. Sign In to your Amazon Prime account. You will be required to log into your Amazon Prime account. Log in using your Amazon Prime subscription's email address and password. Note that you must first create an account if you don't already have one to continue.
  • 3. Note Down The SIX-Digit code displayed (Alternatively, you scan the QR code, if given) After signing in, an alphanumeric code of six to eight letters will be displayed on your Smart TV screen. Therefore, take note of the alphanumeric code displayed on the screen, as you will need to enter it during the activation process.
  • 4. Go to the Amazon MyTV activation page. Using any other device, such as a computer, tablet, or smartphone, open a web browser and visit the Amazon MyTV activation page at amazon.com/mytv. Open a web browser (e.g., Chrome, Safari, Firefox).
  • 5. Login To Your Amazon Account On the Amazon MyTV activation page, you will see a login section. Enter the email address and password you used to sign in on your Smart TV to log into your Amazon account.
  • 6. Enter the six-digit activation code. Once logged in, you will see a space to enter the activation code. Enter the alphanumeric code displayed on your Smart TV screen. Simply type in the alphanumeric code you noted from your Smart TV screen.
  • 7. Double-check the code for accuracy, then click "Activate" or "Submit." Before proceeding, carefully review the activation code you entered, making sure it matches the one displayed on your Smart TV. If the code you entered is accurate, click the "Activate" or "Submit" button to initiate the activation process.
  • 8. Wait for a few minutes. After clicking the activation button, give the system a few moments to verify and process the code. Once the code is successfully validated, your Smart TV will be activated with Amazon MyTV.
  • 9. And That's it; you can now start streaming. Once the activation process is finished, go back to your Smart TV and access the Amazon Prime Video app. You can now browse and enjoy all the content available on the App from the comfort of your Smart TV.
